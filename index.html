FOLIO

Folio strives to be the unique cloud storage application meant to help teachers and students stay organized at school, home, and anywhere in between. This case study takes a look at each step of the design process.

BUSY BUS

For many, public transportation is not easy
or convenient. The Busy Bus project surveyed
people to find out what was needed for a
transportation app to inspire the use of bus
transit. The results led to the addition of a few
key design features that help users better
understand and navigate public transportation.

KSR

Sometimes we wish our favorite apps functioned
a bit differently or had a feature that was
less confusing. I redesigned the mobile landing
screen and blog navigation of Kentucky Sports Radio.
Increased readability was the primary goal of the
redesign.
